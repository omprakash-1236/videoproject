 What is the difference between live and evergreen webinar?

A Live Webinar is a one-time webinar where attendees can watch a webinar Live followed by a Q&A session with the speaker.

An Automated Webinar is an evergreen Webinar where the attendees can watch the webinar for a prearranged period of time together with a 24*7 Q&A Forum open to all attendees, no matter the time.
